Tango

Show your pet the world

Project Overivew

THE PROBLEM

Current travel apps don’t consider pet owners who want to travel with their pets often don’t because of the concern for their pet’s emotional wellbeing and comfort and the additional financial and logistical planning.

THE ASK

How might we help traveling pet owners feel emotionally and logistically organized and prepared when planning to travel?

MY ROLE
